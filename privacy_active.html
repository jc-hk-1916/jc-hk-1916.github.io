Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2025

NaviTerm App Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the NaviTerm application available on the Apple App Store.

1. Information We Collect

NaviTerm does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. All features, including subscription validation, are handled securely via Apple's StoreKit and are processed locally on your device.

2. Subscription Validation

NaviTerm checks your subscription status using StoreKit APIs on the device. No user data or receipts are uploaded to any external server.

3. Local Data Storage

SSH keys, connection profiles, and user-configured settings are stored locally on your device and never leave your device. We have no access to this data.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or cloud services. There is no tracking, logging, or remote syncing involved.

5. Your Rights

Since no data is collected or stored externally, there is no data to request, modify, or delete. All your data remains under your complete control on your device.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@trivix.cn

NaviTerm 应用隐私政策

本隐私政策适用于在 Apple App Store 上架的 NaviTerm 应用程序。

1. 我们收集哪些信息

NaviTerm 不会收集、存储或传输任何可识别用户身份的信息。包括订阅校验在内的所有功能均通过 Apple 的 StoreKit 在设备本地处理。

2. 订阅验证

应用使用 StoreKit 接口在本地验证用户的订阅状态。不上传用户数据或收据信息到任何第三方服务器。

3. 本地数据存储

SSH 密钥、主机配置等用户数据仅保存在用户设备上，从不上传至云端。我们无法访问这些数据。

4. 第三方服务

本应用未接入任何第三方统计、广告或云服务。不包含追踪、不记录日志、不进行远程同步。

5. 用户权利

由于我们不收集任何数据，用户无须担心数据访问、更改或删除问题。您的所有数据完全由您在设备上控制。

6. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请发送邮件至：support@trivix.cn
