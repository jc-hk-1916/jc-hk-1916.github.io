Explain this nginx error to me

Your upstream PHP-FPM socket isn't responding. Three likely causes:

1. PHP-FPM crashed (OOM)

2. Wrong socket path in nginx.conf

3. Permissions on the socket file

systemctl restart php-fpm sysctl vm.overcommit_memory=1

FAQ

Frequently asked
questions

Is NaviTerm really free?

Yes. The free tier supports up to 5 hosts with all core features — SSH, SFTP, port forwarding, snippets, and bring-your-own AI keys. Pro just unlocks unlimited hosts and offline mode.

How much does Pro actually cost?

$0.99/month, $5.99/year (50% off vs monthly), or $29.99 once for lifetime. The lifetime price breaks even with the yearly plan in about 5 years — and includes every future update.

Is the Lifetime deal really lifetime?

Yes — one payment, all future versions of NaviTerm forever. It's tied to your Apple ID. As long as you can re-download the app from the App Store, your Pro features stay active.

Are my SSH keys safe?

Absolutely. Private keys are stored in the iOS / macOS Keychain and never leave your device. We collect zero data — no telemetry, no analytics on your sessions.

Which key types are supported?

RSA, ED25519, P256 ECDSA, and OpenSSH-format encrypted private keys. Password authentication and keyboard-interactive (2FA) are also supported.

How does cross-device sync work?

NaviTerm uses CloudKit (iCloud) — your data syncs end-to-end through your Apple ID. No NaviTerm account, no NaviTerm servers. Toggle it off any time in Settings.

Will background SSH drain my battery?

We use iOS background tasks and BGProcessingTask APIs efficiently. Idle SSH sessions consume negligible power, comparable to a music app paused in the background.

Can I import my hosts from Termius?

Yes — Termius export-to-JSON and OpenSSH config import are both supported. Most users finish migration in under 5 minutes.

Does the AI Copilot cost extra?

No. NaviTerm itself doesn't charge for AI. You just plug in your own OpenAI / Anthropic / Gemini API key and pay providers directly — typically a few cents per month for normal use.

Which AI providers are supported?

OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, and any OpenAI-compatible endpoint (LocalAI, Groq, DeepSeek, OpenRouter, etc.).
