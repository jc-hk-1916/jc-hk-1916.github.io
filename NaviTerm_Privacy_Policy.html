NaviTerm Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

🛡️ NaviTerm App Privacy Policy (App Store Version)

Effective Date: June 1, 2025

1. What Information We Collect

NaviTerm does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. All features, including subscription validation, are handled securely via Apple’s StoreKit and are processed locally on the device.

2. Subscription Validation

NaviTerm checks the user’s subscription status using StoreKit APIs on the device. No user data or receipts are uploaded to any external server.

3. Local Data

SSH keys, connection profiles, and user-configured settings are stored locally on your device and never leave your device.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or cloud services. There is no tracking, logging, or remote syncing involved.

5. Your Rights

Since no data is collected or stored externally, there is no data to request, modify, or delete.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, feel free to contact us at: support@trivix.cn

🛡️ NaviTerm 应用隐私政策（App Store 版本）

生效日期：2025年6月1日

1. 我们收集哪些信息

NaviTerm 不会收集、存储或传输任何可识别用户身份的信息。包括订阅校验在内的所有功能均通过 Apple 的 StoreKit 在设备本地处理。

2. 订阅验证

应用使用 StoreKit 接口在本地验证用户的订阅状态。不上传用户数据或收据信息到任何第三方服务器。

3. 本地数据

SSH 密钥、主机配置等用户数据仅保存在用户设备上，从不上传至云端。

4. 第三方服务

本应用未接入任何第三方统计、广告或云服务。不包含追踪、不记录日志、不进行远程同步。

5. 用户权利

由于我们不收集任何数据，用户无须担心数据访问、更改或删除问题。

6. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请发送邮件至：support@trivix.cn
